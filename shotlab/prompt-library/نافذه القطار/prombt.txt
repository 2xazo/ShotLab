Create a premium 10-second cinematic product commercial inspired by a **“Magical Train Window Moment.”** Use the attached product as the exact visual reference and seamlessly place the real product into a luxurious train environment.

## Core Concept

The commercial captures a quiet, magical travel moment inside an elegant moving train. The product rests beside the window while a breathtaking, dreamlike landscape slowly passes outside.

The result should feel emotional, luxurious, poetic, and cinematic, while remaining physically believable and grounded in real-world photography.

## Product Accuracy — Highest Priority

The attached product must remain completely identical to the reference image throughout the entire video.

Do not modify, redesign, improve, simplify, enhance, stylize, or reinterpret any part of the product.

Preserve exactly:

* Packaging
* Shape and proportions
* Logo and branding
* Label and typography
* Colors
* Materials and textures
* Printed details
* Reflections and gloss
* Edges and manufacturing details
* Natural imperfections
* Every visible design element

Do not replace the product with an AI-generated recreation, approximation, or redesigned version.

Treat the attached product as the real physical object and composite it seamlessly into the scene. It must remain perfectly consistent, readable, undistorted, and unchanged in every frame.

* Duration: 10 seconds
* Premium cinematic commercial

## Product Placement

Place the product naturally on one of the following surfaces inside the train cabin:

* An elegant train window ledge

## Shot Sequence

### 0–2 Seconds — Introduction

Fade in slowly from black.

A gentle cinematic push-in reveals the product resting beside the train window.

Warm cabin lighting softly illuminates the product while the train moves with subtle, realistic vibrations and gentle swaying.

Natural reflections should appear on the window glass and polished surfaces without obscuring the product.

### 2–5 Seconds — Cinematic Reveal

Perform a smooth, slow cinematic orbit around the product.

Outside the train window, an enchanted but believable landscape moves naturally with the train journey.

The scenery may include:

* Glowing countryside
* Mist-covered mountains
* Shimmering lakes
* Magical forests
* Floating clouds
* Tiny distant villages
* Golden sunset light
* Soft moonlit landscapes

The exterior movement must follow realistic train parallax and perspective.

It should feel like a real train travelling through an extraordinary world, not like an animated fantasy sequence.

### 5–8 Seconds — Focus and Atmosphere

Use an elegant rack focus between the perfectly sharp product and the soft landscape outside the window.

Add subtle environmental details such as:

* Gentle window condensation
* Natural glass reflections
* Soft atmospheric haze
* Fine floating dust particles
* Slight sunlight changes
* Subtle exposure adaptation
* Authentic train vibrations

The product must remain visually dominant, accurate, and fully recognizable throughout the focus transition.

### 8–10 Seconds — Final Hero Shot

Settle into a refined hero composition.

The train continues moving through the magical landscape while warm natural light wraps gently around the untouched product.

The camera becomes almost still, with only subtle movement from the train.

End on an elegant premium advertising frame that feels timeless, emotional, and visually memorable.
